Efficient Text-to-Speech Synthesis with Retraining-Free Pruning for Speaker Cloning

Authors: Ali Raheem Mandeel, Mohammed Salah Al-Radhi

Email: {alimandeel, malradhi}@tmit.bme.hu

Abstract

This study explores cross-lingual speaker adaptation techniques for Arabic speech synthesis, utilizing various TTS models and vocoders. The synthesized speech quality is evaluated for the target speaker, using different models and configurations.

Datasets

  • Arabic Speech Corpus
  • One male target speaker
